Where am I?

Last updated August 2020

I tend to compartmentalize my various trains of thought online. This constraint generally seems to suit me, so I formalized the idea into online personas that incorporate various of my tangential interests. Each persona represents a sustained train of thought—in some cases delineated temporally.

Until now, I haven’t tried visualizing the constellations of social media personas that have arisen from this delineation of interests. I was inspired to make such a diagram after seeing Yehwan Song’s visualization of her creative projects during her presentation at Typographics 2020.

I'm often in many places at once.
